OVERVIEW

Godrej Prakriti is spread across 22.9 acres of lush greenery and is urbanized to the core. Infused with natural tranquility, 1, 2 and 3 BHK as per the various lifestyle preferences. Resort style living where 75% open space along with 4.68 acres of virgin green landscape evolving 1400+ old trees spread over 20000 sq ft of resort club with expansive lounge, swimming pool added with 6000 sq ft gazebo. Fully equipped multi gym with 20000 sq ft football ground and 12000 sq ft of children's playground. 871 mtrs of walking trails and 50000 sq ft of retail plaza fully operational. With the majestic Hooghly River close by, this complex is endowed with strong breeze and spectacular views - turning your home into a perfect retreat.
